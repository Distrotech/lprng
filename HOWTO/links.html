Printer Manufacturers

Hewlett-Packard

Main Web Site http://www.hp.com
Support Web Site http://support.hp.com or http://www.hp.com/cposupport/eschome.html

The Developers Connection site http://www.hp-developer-solutions.com offers support for the various products. Registration required. Also has access to a $500/year enhanced support program.

If you want information about PCL, PJL or others, register and then go to the Developers Kits and Technical Information link. You will need to select the printer family for which you want the information, and then fight your way down the links. Search for the information by title may or may not work.

The http://www.hp-laserjet-developers.com/ site also has a lot of reference material for the laserjet printer.
